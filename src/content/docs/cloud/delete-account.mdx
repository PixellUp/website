# Delete account

Navigate to **Account** and click on the **Data** tab.

<img src="https://static.umami.dev/website/images/cloud/account-data.png" />

Fill out the form details and click the **Delete** button.

<img src="https://static.umami.dev/website/images/cloud/account-delete-form.png" />

Your account will now be deleted. You may sign up with the same email in the future.
